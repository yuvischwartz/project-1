ORGANIC CEYLON CINNAMON

Not all cinnamon is created equally. Ceylon cinnamon is rich
in antioxidants, has been found to reduce blood sugar levels,
reduce heart disease risk factors, and has been used as
an herbal supplement dating as far back as Ancient Egypt!

Our Organic Ceylon cinnamon is carefully sourced, giving
you the highest-quality and most aromatic organic product
on the market. Cinnamon is a versatile ingredient and can be
used on anything sweet or savory. Add a dash to your
oatmeal, bake with it, or add it to your vegetables or proteins;
there are SO many ways to spice up your life with our Ceylon
Cinnamon.
