FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


- Are Yuvron products gluten free?

Answer: At Yuvron Naturals we offer gluten free product

- Are Yuvron products vegan?

Answer: All of our products are 100% vegan

-How are your products roasted?

Answer: We oil roast our products with canola oil at our own and other facilities here in the US.

-Do you have coupons?

Answer: We do offer coupons on our website

-Do you sell one pound bags?

Answer: Currently we do not offer one pound bags but we are looking forward to having it in the future!

-When will my order ship?

Answer: Standard ground shipping is offered by UPS and typically takes 1-5 business days to deliver, depending on location and product mix.
We offer UPS 2 Day (guaranteed delivery in 2 days) and USPS Priority (delivery in 1-3 business days). Orders typically ship within 48 hours of receipt.

-How do I track my order?

Answer: We provide tracking information that allows you to access the location of your Yuvron package. (we have to decide with which carrier we will ship them with - tomorrow)

-What if my package does not arrive?

Answer: We have 24 hour customer service responders that will help you identify where your package is. Just give us a call at 1800-888-888 (until we have a number) or email us at hello@yuvron.com !

-Where can you ship to? Do you ship internationally?

Answer: Currently we do shipping inside the US only but are looking forward to international shipping in the near future!

-Can I place Wholesale orders?

Answer: Currently we do not offer wholesale orders, but we are looking forward to having it in the future!

-Do you offer refunds or replacements?

Answer: Currently only offer replacements if there is damage to the packaging due to Covid-19!
